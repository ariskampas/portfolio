About me

I was born and raised in Greece, at a time when home computers were becoming available, and immediately I became interested in the magical world of computers. I saw that technology, thoughtfully designed and carefully executed, has the capacity to improve the human experience.

I made my mission to listen. My goal is to understand the complexity of systems and the people that use them. My work is inspired by humans and created for humans to improve the human condition. I apply design thinking to find solutions that give great value to users and other stake-holders alike. My strongest skillsets are web design, research, user testing, business strategy, market research, and visuals design.

If I had to describe myself using one word, it would be ‘multipotentialite‘ and here is why: I am passionate about a wide variety of subjects, that inspire me and bring out the best of my abilities. I’m highly self-driven, always keen to acquire new knowledge and competences, and get along great with all types of people.

My background combined with my work and life experiences and my love for innovation, brings along a strong work ethic, reliability and a strong basis for a stable working environment. This also makes me a strong asset in cross-cultural teams and in out-of-the-box-thinking environments in where I thrive.

I am currently open to new opportunities and design projects.
